Chika Fujiwara

Chika Fujiwara (藤原 千花ふじわら ちか, Fujiwara Chika) is the heroine in the Kaguya-sama wa Kokurasetai series. She is a second-year high school student at Shuchi'in Academy, a member of the Tabletop Gaming Club, and the current secretary of the high school Student Council.
